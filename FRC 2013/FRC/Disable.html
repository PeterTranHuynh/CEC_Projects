Disable VI Template Appearance

When first opening the Disable VI and enter it's block diagram, it should appear to look something like the picture to the left. The Diable VI is responsible for deactivating all motors, or recalibrating anything on the robot to default or initial settings. For example, Disable can be used to reposition the camera back to it's original position and stopping all motors when the robot is shut off.

The template has provided a small example of the code, stopping the intial drive motors when the robot is deactivated.

Extending Disable.VI

Simply add motors you need to stop when the robot is deactivated, and peripheral devices connected to the robot that you need repositioned. As shown in the picture, our team added three motor stops to this VI.
